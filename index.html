UK Sovereign Capability

Built in Britain.
Owned by Britain.

The UK government's strategic posture, the National Security and Investment Act 2021, and NCSC supply chain guidance all point in the same direction: security-critical technology deployed at UK CNI and defence sites should, where possible, be designed, built and operated by UK entities. Canopy is built to that standard from the ground up.

  • NSI

    National Security and Investment Act 2021

    UK-designed, UK-built, UK-operated. No supply chain dependency, no licensing risk, no capability withdrawal risk from a foreign government.

  • NCSC

    NCSC Supply Chain Compliance

    Cyber Essentials Plus pathway. CREST penetration tested. Zero Trust architecture. AWS GovCloud UK with contractual UK data residency.

  • CAP

    CAP 2563 and Regulatory Compliance

    Legally governed from first principles. The ROE engine makes non-compliant activation architecturally impossible. All RF operations are pursued through the correct Ofcom regulatory pathway before any transmission.

  • CPS

    Court-Admissible Evidence Framework

    Every engagement generates a SHA-256 chained evidence package meeting CPS standards for prosecution under the Civil Aviation Act 1982 and Misuse of Drugs Act 1971.
