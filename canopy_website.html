The UK government's strategic posture, the National Security and Investment Act 2021, and NCSC supply chain guidance all point in the same direction: security-critical technology deployed at UK CNI and defence sites should, where possible, be designed, built and operated by UK entities. Canopy is built to that standard from the ground up.

  • NSI

    National Security and Investment Act 2021

    UK-designed, UK-built, UK-operated. No supply chain dependency, no licensing risk, no capability withdrawal risk from a foreign government.

    CAP 2563 and Regulatory Compliance
